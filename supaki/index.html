SUPAKI

Project Type : Android App
Organisation : SUPAKI
Team : Me and the stakeholders

Android App is live on Google play Store in Indonesia and Philippines

Project Overview

SUPAKI is a versatile eCommerce solution for mobile games that supports direct-to-consumer in-game item sales as well as successful player engagement techniques. SUPAKI is a comprehensive system that offers online stores to game development companies. SUPAKI enables these companies to sell in-game items to gamers, offer personalised promos, and even give away prizes. SUPAKI, as a third-party solution, is more than simply an Android app; it is a suite of products that provide a cost-effective and efficient means for companies to operate their stores outside of the game, reducing the need for internal development. Players can quickly purchase certain things via the SUPAKI Android app and have them delivered directly to their in-game accounts.

Apart from that, SUPAKI is an innovative mobile application that caters to the diverse needs of the gaming community. With a focus on blockchain and non-blockchain games, through curated recommendations and personalised suggestions, users can discover new and exciting gaming experiences tailored to their preferences. SUPAKI provides users with a comprehensive platform to explore, trade, analyse, and engage with the gaming world like never before.

Target Audience

Enthusiastic gamers and blockchain gaming enthusiasts ranging from casual players to hardcore collectors looking for a comprehensive and interactive gaming platform.

User pain points & Objectives

  1. Provide a user-friendly interface for exploring both blockchain and non-blockchain games.
  2. Users are unable to generate real-world value from their in-game achievements and items. So, creating a secure and streamlined trading platform for buying and selling in-game items.
  3. Enable users to analyse the dynamic in-game item market for informed decision-making.
  4. Users miss out on opportunities to engage with a broader community and share their gaming experiences and achievements. Currently, users don’t have a platform where they can do all at one place. So, facilitating personal expression by allowing users to showcase their bought items and achievements.
  5. Support user-generated content by integrating features for uploading streams and videos.
  6. Foster community interaction through a dedicated page for posting items and engaging with other users.

Key Features

  1. Home Page : SUPAKI’s intuitive interface allows users to seamlessly explore a vast collection of both blockchain and non-blockchain games. Through curated recommendations and personalised suggestions, users can discover new and exciting gaming experiences tailored to their preferences. Users can discover the games on the basis of genre, type of in-game items, similar games they usually play.
  2. Market Analysis : Empowering users with data-driven insights, SUPAKI includes a dedicated section for item market analysis. Users can track the value trends of in-game items, enabling informed decisions when trading. Real-time analytics and historical data contribute to a comprehensive understanding of the dynamic in-game item market.
  3. Trade (Buy and Sell) In-Game Items : The heart of SUPAKI lies in its robust trading platform. Users can buy and sell in-game items securely using blockchain technology, ensuring transparency and authenticity. The integrated marketplace facilitates seamless transactions, allowing gamers to enhance their gaming experience by acquiring rare and coveted items.
  4. Community Page for Interactions : SUPAKI has introduced a unique feature allowing users to showcase their acquired items through personalised profiles, sharing achievements and rare finds with the gaming community. This community page serves as a hub for connecting, discussing, and interacting, promoting community building and making SUPAKI more than just a store app. Users can post items, share gaming strategies, and engage in conversations with like-minded individuals.
  5. Upload Streams/Videos for Games : Enabling users to share their gaming experiences, SUPAKI incorporates a seamless video upload and streaming feature. Users can upload gameplay videos, and walkthroughs directly through the app, fostering a vibrant gaming community and providing valuable content for others to enjoy.

Launched the SUPAKI app on Google play store in Indonesia and Philippines, providing users with a feature-rich platform that seamlessly blends blockchain and non-blockchain gaming experiences.
