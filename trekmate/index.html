Trekmate

OVERVIEW

TrekMate is an app that enables you to journey independently, that is, without the assistance of a guide or other person. You only need a smartphone with GPS and a sufficient battery, of course.
With improved weather and doable conditions, it aids in helping you plan your hike appropriately. It explains the ideal time of year to go on the journey.

ROLE

User Experience, User Research, Prototyping, Interaction, User Interface Design

TIMELINE

October 2019 - November 2019

GOAL

  1. The primary difficulty for a trekker or hiker is usually figuring out how to carry on when he or she doesn't know the trail or route; here is where the app comes in handy.
  2. This app's sole purpose is to assist a trekker or hiker in completing the trek independently, without the aid of a guide or other individual who can provide adequate planning.

CHALLENGE

  1. How to compile or gather specific information about the treks.
  2. How to convey this information to a novice hiker or untrained trekker in an approachable and understandable manner.

ASSUMPTION

  1. Have all the information on treks, including a map, coordinates (start and end points), basecamps, sources of fresh water, neighboring villages, etc., that can be gathered by different organizations.
  2. Treks that are published in the app must not be untried treks; a trekker may only trek on a trail that has already been traversed once.

SOLUTION

The thought of landmarks entered my head. Since they are useful in daily life as well, why not in trekking? What can be done is to list the landmarks and the direction of the trail or route. a map providing all the information a trekker needs, such as the location of the basecamp, any neighboring villages, the location of a water supply, etc. Also included is any information on landslide-prone areas or similar. The user will be informed by notification of the timing of the trip, including whether or not to start, whether or not to move back, the optimal time to summit, and when he must return to basecamps.
The secret to a successful hike or summit is to stick to the schedule.

KEY FEATURES

  1. Provides basecamp, water source, and any nearby village locations.
  2. provides landmarks that aid in navigation alongside the map. In order to determine whether the user is on the correct trail or not, it is possible to question him or her if they have passed a particular landmark or not.
